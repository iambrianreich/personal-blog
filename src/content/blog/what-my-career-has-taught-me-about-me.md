They say *"do what you love, and you'll never work a day in your life."* Life
experience makes me wonder about **"they"**:

* How did they know what they *love*?
* How was their life so *damn free* that toiling at work they didn't enjoy was
  completely avoidable?

The last decade has been a journey. Considering it's a road I'm likely stuck on
for another 20 years, it felt wise to check in with myself and see if I'm still
walking in the right direction.

at an agency and left it within a year for several reasons:

  make it work, via early-morning and late-night heroics.

  CMS, built by lead developer/co-owner, which had a tremendous amount of
  "toil" baked into every build.
